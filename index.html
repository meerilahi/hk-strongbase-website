About HK StrongBase Builders

HK StrongBase Builders (SMC Private) Limited

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional infrastructure solutions for government and public sector projects through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to safety and compliance standards.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Pakistan's most trusted and reliable construction partner for government infrastructure development, setting benchmarks in quality, transparency, and timely project delivery.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality Excellence, Safety First, Transparency in Operations, Environmental Responsibility, and Commitment to Deadlines. We build not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients.

Leadership & Founder

Guided by Experience, Driven by Excellence

Hakeem Khan

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With extensive experience in civil construction and infrastructure development, Hakeem Khan founded HK StrongBase Builders with a vision to transform Pakistan's infrastructure landscape. His leadership has been instrumental in establishing the company as a trusted partner for government and public sector projects.

Under his guidance, HK StrongBase Builders has successfully completed numerous projects in sewerage systems, drainage networks, road construction, and general civil engineering works, maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance.

Hakeem Khan's commitment to excellence, transparency, and timely delivery has earned the company recognition and trust among government departments and public sector organizations across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and beyond.
